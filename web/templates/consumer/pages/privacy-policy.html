Landlords of Texas, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our property management services.

1. Overview

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that any personal information you provide is handled responsibly and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for services, schedule property tours, submit inquiries, or communicate with us. This information may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Tour booking preferences
  • Communication history

Automatically Collected Information

When you access our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Process tour bookings and inquiries
  • Communicate about properties and services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

Property Owners and Landlords

When you express interest in a property or submit a rental application, we share relevant information with property owners and landlords to facilitate the rental process.

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting business, and providing services to you.

Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure storage systems, regular security assessments, and employee training on data protection practices.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to maintaining industry-standard protections.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, personalize content, and help our agents provide better service. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies (Always Active): Required for site functionality, authentication, security features, and session management. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function.
  • Analytics & Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website through metrics like page views, time on site, bounce rate, and traffic sources. We use tools like Google Analytics to collect this information.
  • Behavioral Intelligence Cookies: Track your property viewing behavior, search patterns, and interactions to help our agents understand your preferences and provide personalized recommendations. This includes:
    • Properties you view and how long you view them
    • Search criteria and filters you use
    • Properties you save or inquire about
    • Showing requests and application submissions
    • Email engagement (opens, clicks)
    • Session duration and page navigation patterns

    Integration: This data is synced with our CRM system (FollowUpBoss) to help agents prioritize leads, provide faster responses, and offer relevant property suggestions based on your interests.

  • Marketing & Communication Cookies: Used to show you relevant property updates, market information, and email campaigns based on your preferences and viewing history. These help us send you personalized property alerts and market updates.

How We Track Behavioral Data

When you use our website, we may collect the following behavioral data:

  • Browsing Activity: Pages visited, time spent on each page, navigation paths
  • Property Interactions: Properties viewed, saved, compared, or inquired about
  • Search Behavior: Search terms, filters applied, location preferences
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device fingerprint, IP address
  • Session Data: Login times, session duration, return visits
  • Geolocation: General location data (city/region level) to show relevant properties

Why We Use Behavioral Tracking

  • Personalized Service: Help agents understand your preferences and respond more effectively
  • Lead Scoring: Prioritize serious buyers/renters for faster agent response times
  • Property Recommendations: Suggest properties that match your interests
  • User Experience: Improve website navigation and functionality
  • Marketing Optimization: Send relevant property alerts and market updates

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have full control over which cookies to accept. You can:

  • Change Settings: Click "Cookie Preferences" in the footer to update your choices at any time
  • Opt Out of Behavioral Tracking: Disable behavioral intelligence cookies to prevent tracking of your property viewing behavior
  • Clear Cookies: Use your browser settings to delete existing cookies
  • Browser Controls: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies entirely, though this may limit website functionality

Data Retention

  • Behavioral Events: Stored for 24 months, then automatically deleted
  • Session Data: Retained for 90 days
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated data retained indefinitely; individual data deleted after 26 months
  • Cookie Consent Records: Retained for 3 years for compliance purposes

Your Privacy Matters: If you opt out of behavioral tracking, we will not collect data about your property viewing behavior. However, this means our agents will have less information to provide personalized service, and you may not receive relevant property recommendations.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request corrections or updates to inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data and behavioral tracking history
  • Right to Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications and disable behavioral tracking
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing, including profiling and direct marketing
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request temporary suspension of data processing in certain circumstances

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Right to delete personal information held by businesses
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@propertyhub.com
  • Subject Line: "Privacy Rights Request"
  • Include: Your name, email address, and specific request

We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request to protect your privacy and security.

Data Deletion Requests

When you request data deletion, we will:

  • Delete all behavioral tracking data associated with your account
  • Remove your personal information from our CRM system
  • Anonymize any data required for legal or accounting purposes
  • Confirm deletion via email within 30 days

Note: Some information may be retained if required by law, for fraud prevention, or to complete transactions you requested.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
